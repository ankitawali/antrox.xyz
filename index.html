About Us

Radiojitter is into IoT based solution, antenna design, software-defined radio sale and research. We are working LoRaWAN based solution (Gateway and Sensor) for smart city solution. Our areas of interests are RF sensors, gateway development and provide end to end solution in LoRaWAN including network simulationand spectrum planning. Radiojitter provides training and consulting in IoT RF security. We have an e-commerce division which deals in embedded hobby electronics, radio products, and HAM/Amateur radio accessories. We are authorized reseller of multiple OEMs including RTL-SDR Blog, Nooelec, SDRplay, HackRF, DORJI, and NiceRF.
